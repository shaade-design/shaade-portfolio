Principal-level designer turning complex systems into clear, human experiences.

I'm a product designer with 10+ years of experience shaping enterprise and consumer products. I thrive at the intersection of data, systems, and craft — designing things that help people make sense of complexity and do their best work.

My approach is grounded in curiosity and research. I'm constantly testing ideas, listening to users, and refining designs to make products easier to use, more efficient, and more human. Lately I've been especially interested in how AI can reduce friction in knowledge work — without losing the human in the loop.

I've mentored early-career designers through Triangle UXPA and I'm a proud graduate of NC State's College of Design. Outside of work, I enjoy staying active, traveling, and finding inspiration in everyday details.
