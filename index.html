About

Designing with intention.

I'm a product designer focused on SaaS and data-intensive products — turning complex systems into clear, intuitive experiences that help people do their best work.

I've spent my career partnering closely with cross-functional teams to shape product direction, uncover real user needs, and design scalable solutions. NC State College of Design grad.

Capabilities

UI/UX Design AI Product Design Product Strategy Design Systems User Research Prototyping Data Visualization Enterprise UX

Approach

My approach is grounded in curiosity, research, and craft. I'm constantly testing ideas, listening to users, and refining designs to make products easier to use, more efficient, and more human.

Lately, I've been especially interested in how products can respect people's time and emotions — particularly in moments when they're seeking help or clarity.
